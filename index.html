About Me

Growing up I always had a passion for computers and technology. I loved playing games with my friends and this passion evolved into a curiosity for how the technology I use so often works. In high school I taught myself Python and began to explore the world of coding. I quickly fell in love with the problem solving aspect of coding and the endless possibilities that come with it, and I knew that I wanted to pursue a career in software engineering.

I've since made the leap to turn my passion into a career by attending a coding bootcamp. At this bootcamp I learned JavaScript, React, Redux, Express, Flask, and SQLAlchemy. My time spent at this bootcamp has only deepened my passion and love for programming. I spent a great amount of time collaborating with other software engineers, allowing us to help each other grow and improve as engineers. As of now, I've developed three full stack clones of Airbnb, Yelp, and Brainscape.
